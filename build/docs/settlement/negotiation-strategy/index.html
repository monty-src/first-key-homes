Leverage Points​

  • Treble damages: $147,210
  • Plus: $15,000-$50,000 punitive
  • Plus: Attorney fees and costs
  • Total: $160,000+

Jury Appeal​

  • Perfect tenants (3-year payment history)
  • Professional credibility (SVP + Engineer)
  • Child endangerment (51 hours at 57°F)
  • Clear bad faith (multiple broken promises)
  • Perfect documentation

Timeline Pressure​

  • 48-hour settlement deadline
  • December holiday season
  • Christmas disruption (media angle)
  • Regulatory complaints pending

Reputational Risk​

  • Media story: "SVP at Bank of America family endangered"
  • NC Real Estate Commission complaint
  • Code enforcement violations
  • BetterBusiness Bureau report

Phase 1: Final Demand (Dec 13, 8 PM Deadline)​

Demand: $35,000 Justification: Below $147,210 treble exposure Alternative: $25,000 minimum, all terms

  • Respond in writing
  • Can negotiate within ranges

Phase 2: If No Settlement (Dec 16+)​

File Lawsuit

  • Request $147,210 treble
  • Add punitive damages claim
  • List attorney fees
  • Total: $160,000+

File Complaints

  • NC Real Estate Commission

Media Contact

  • Explain case facts (not settlements)

What FirstKey Will Consider​

FirstKey likely knows:

  1. They will lose - liability is clear
  2. Damages will be high - documentation is complete
  3. Treble damages exposure - $147,210+ is likely
  4. Jury appeal is strong - child endangerment + perfect tenants
  5. Media risk - negative PR on landlord violations
  6. Regulatory risk - licenses/certifications at stake
  7. Settlement is cheaper - $35,000 vs. $160,000+

FirstKey's incentive: Settle for $35,000

Court Process:

  1. File lawsuit (Dec 16)
  2. Service on FirstKey
  3. Initial hearing (30-60 days)
  4. Discovery (documents, depositions)
  5. Mediation attempt
  6. Trial (if no settlement)

Court will find FirstKey liable 99% probability

Jury will likely award high damages due to:

  • Child endangerment
  • Multiple broken promises
  • Perfect tenant credibility
  • Vendor confirmation of needed repairs
  • 73+ days of pattern
