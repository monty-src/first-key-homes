# Additional Legal Claims

In addition to statutory violations, FirstKey faces common law claims:

## Breach of Contract
- Lease requires habitable premises
- FirstKey failed this material term
- Damages directly caused

## Negligence
- Duty: Maintain safe premises
- Breach: Failed maintenance
- Causation: Clear
- Damages: $49,070+

## Negligent Infliction of Emotional Distress
- Extreme/outrageous conduct (child endangerment)
- Severe emotional distress (3 months documented)
- Foreseeable harm
- Severe damages

## Constructive Eviction
- 57°F uninhabitable
- Child had to be removed
- Tenant right to terminate without penalty

## Child Endangerment
- Knowing endangerment
- 70+ hours at 57°F
- Refused safe alternative
- Inadequate solution offered
