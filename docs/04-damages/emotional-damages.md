# Emotional Distress Damages

## Quantified Emotional Impact

- **3 months of stress**: $6,000
- **Child safety anxiety**: $3,000
- **Depression/mental health**: $2,000
- **Loss of home enjoyment**: $2,000
- **Sleep disruption**: $2,000

## Total: $15,000

## Justification

NC law recognizes damages for emotional distress caused by:
- Knowing child endangerment
- Multiple broken promises
- 3-month ongoing crisis
- Separation from family during holidays
- Loss of peace and safety in home
