# Settlement Options

## Primary: $35,000

**Best balance:**
- Covers actual damages + professional impact
- Significantly below treble exposure
- Reasonable incentive for settlement
- Includes all key terms

## Option A: $28,000 + Stay

**If tenants want to stay:**
- Lower cash payment
- HVAC fixed immediately
- 3 months rent reduction (50%)
- Ability to terminate anytime

## Option B: $40,000 + Move

**If tenants want to leave:**
- Higher cash payment
- Covers moving costs
- Clean break
- Deposit returned immediately

## Option C: $50,000 Full Demand

**To avoid litigation:**
- Complete settlement
- All terms included
- Expedited payment
- Confidentiality agreement
