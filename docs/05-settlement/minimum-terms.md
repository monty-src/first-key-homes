# Minimum Acceptable Terms

## Financial

**$25,000 MINIMUM CASH**
- Below this: lawsuit filed immediately
- Non-negotiable floor

**Outstanding $477.16 Credit**
- FirstKey already owes this
- Must be paid in full

---

## Property & Lease

**HVAC Repair Timeline (Specific Dates)**
- Must begin: December [X]
- Must complete: December [X]
- Vague promises unacceptable
- Professional contractor required

**Lease Termination Without Penalty**
- Clean break required
- No non-compliance fees
- No eviction-related reporting

**Full Deposit Return**
- All funds returned
- With interest
- Within stated timeframe

---

## Legal & Documentation

**All Terms in Writing**
- Signed settlement agreement
- No verbal agreements
- Copy to tenant attorney

---

## Deal Breakers

**Absolutely WILL NOT accept:**

1. **Payment Plans**
   - Need lump sum
   - No installments

2. **Vague Promises**
   - "We'll fix it eventually"
   - No specific timeline

3. **Continued Tenancy Without Rent Reduction**
   - If staying, must have 50%+ reduction
   - Otherwise leaving

4. **Non-Disclosure Preventing Reviews**
   - Can disclose facts
   - Cannot silence truth
   - "Mutual NDA" OK for Confidential parts

5. **Waivers of Future Claims**
   - If issues recur, tenant keeps right to sue
   - No blanket release
